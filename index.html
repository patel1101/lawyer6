About -DEBASIS MAJHI

Welcome to DEBASIS MAJHI-Advocates and IPR Attorneys, the best cost-effective and quick execution IPR law firm in India.

DEBASIS MAJHI-Advocates and IPR Attorneys provides worldwide services for the protection of Intellectual Property Rights to valued clients. We specialize in the practice areas of Trademarks,Designs, Copyrights, Patents, Geographical Indications, Semiconductors, Plant Varieties, National Biodiversity, Domain Registrations, Trade Secrets, and more. DEBASIS MAJHI-Advocates and IPR Attorneys is a name to reckon with. No matter what kind of Intellectual Property Rights services you need globally, DEBASIS MAJHI- Advocates and IPR Attorneys and their team are always organized and ready to assist promptly. If you are looking for the most trustworthy IPR law firm in India, do not hesitate to contact us at mailipr@debasismajhi.com and advocate@debasismajhi.com.
